How to make an egg, or rather have it made for you

Description

An egg is a blob of fat and protein, encased by a blob of protein, encased by a porose shell made of calcium and other minerals.

I know this sounds like a lot of work, and it really is. But to be honest, we're not gonna do most of it. Outsourcing.

Ingredients

  • A chicken
  • A nest
  • Not having a fox around
  • Food for the chicken

Steps

  1. Give food for the chicken when it's hungry, or look up how often chickens eat whatever it is they eat.
  2. Wait. Talk to the chicken if you want. Name the chicken, too.
  3. Check under the chicken daily. Your egg might as well be there.
  4. This is magic, really. You're casting an egg spell.
